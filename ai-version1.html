Episode 1 - New Beginnings

Liv stood at the edge of the sprawling campus courtyard, the late afternoon sun casting long shadows across the polished brick paths. Her suitcase felt heavier than it was, nerves pulling her in different directions.

She paused, gazing up at the stone archways that framed the entrance to her dormitory. The prestigious Elmore University, known globally for producing the next generation of leaders and elites, loomed before her. A soft breeze rustled the ivy climbing the walls, carrying faint laughter from distant students.
